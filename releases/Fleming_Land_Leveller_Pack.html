About the Pack

Bring a true Irish touch to your fieldwork with this pack of traditional-style land levellers. Designed for land leveling, soil aeration, and light surface cultivation, these tools are perfect for improving drainage and preparing seedbeds. Whether you're managing a smallholding or a large farm, there's a leveller to suit your needs. This is the Fleming-branded version of the pack — same lineup as the Lizard release, currently waiting on final testing before going out.
