About the Mod

A PTO-powered pressure washer designed to clean tools around the farm. Unlike the original FS22 build, the FS25 version runs on the game's native high-pressure washer specialization, so it's fully functional out of the box — no separate hose purchase required. A hose length configuration is available in the shop.
