Hi, I'm Kelly

About Me

I create Farming Simulator mods and beginner-friendly tutorials that explain the modding process in a simple, easy-to-follow way.

My goal is to help new and experienced modders learn more, try new things, and bring their ideas to life. Whether you're starting from zero or looking to improve your skills, my tutorials are designed to be clear, practical, and straight to the point.

I mainly work with Blender, Giants Editor, and Onshape — and I share everything I learn along the way, including the mistakes.

More tutorials and mod releases are added regularly, so check back often!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any experience to follow your tutorials?
No experience needed at all! My tutorials are written for complete beginners. I explain every step and don't skip anything that might seem obvious.
Which version of Farming Simulator do your tutorials cover?
Most tutorials focus on Farming Simulator 25 (FS25), but the majority of the techniques also work in FS22. I'll always mention if something is version-specific.
Are your mods free to download?
Yes, all of my mods are completely free. You can find them on ModHub or linked directly from the blog release posts.
Can I use your mods in my videos or streams?
Of course! Feel free to use my mods in any videos or streams. A mention or link back is always appreciated but not required.
I'm stuck on something — can you help?
Absolutely, jump into the Discord server and ask there — it's the best place to get help quickly. You can also send me an email if you prefer.
