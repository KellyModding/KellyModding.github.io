About

Hi, I'm Kelly Modding

I create Farming Simulator mods and make beginner-friendly tutorials that explain the modding process in a simple, understandable way.

My goal is to help new and experienced modders learn more, try new things, and bring their ideas to life. Whether you're starting from zero or looking to go further, my tutorials are designed to be easy to follow and straight to the point.

No complicated explanations — just clear steps that make sense. More content is added regularly, so check back often for updates.
